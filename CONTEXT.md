# Barbati la Fain Network

A private, invite-only network (≤1000 people) where members publish their skills and
projects so others can find the right person to help them. Discovery happens through an
in-app AI chat that searches over members.

## Language

**Member**:
A person who has claimed an invite and joined the network. Has a profile and can search
for other members.
_Avoid_: User, account

**Admin**:
A member with rights to generate invites. Created directly in the database, not via the app.
_Avoid_: Moderator, owner

**Invite**:
A single-use code that grants the right to become a Member. Generated by Admins in batches,
sent out of band, and claimed once during sign-up.
_Avoid_: Invitation link, token, voucher

**Profile**:
The information a Member publishes about themselves: name, location, social links, projects,
and skills.
_Avoid_: Bio, page, account

**Skill**:
A capability a Member offers to the network (e.g. "construction with natural materials").
The primary thing the AI search matches on.
_Avoid_: Tag, expertise, service

**Project**:
A piece of work a Member cares about and is involved in. Published on the profile and
searchable alongside skills.
_Avoid_: Initiative, work item

**Directory**:
The browsable listing of all Members. Every Member can see every other Member's full
profile here, including social links. Discovery complement to People Search.
_Avoid_: Roster, member list

**People Search**:
The in-app AI chat where a Member asks in natural language for people who can help, and gets
back matching Members with reasons. Powered by vector search over profiles + an LLM.
_Avoid_: ChatGPT, the bot, assistant
